# Content Policy & Community Standards

**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

⚠️ **IMPORTANT NOTICE**: This document includes temporary DMCA procedures (Section 6) that provide good-faith copyright protection until Legacy Vault registers a designated DMCA agent with the U.S. Copyright Office. For immediate copyright concerns, contact copyright@legacyvault.com.

## 1. Our Commitment

Legacy Vault is a sacred space for memorialization. This policy ensures we maintain dignity, protect families, and comply with legal obligations while balancing the deeply personal nature of remembrance.

**By creating a memorial, you agree to:**
- Only upload content you own or have permission to use
- Respect the privacy and dignity of the deceased and their families
- Indemnify Legacy Vault against disputes arising from your memorial content
- Accept our Family Dispute Resolution procedures

---

## 2. Prohibited Content (Zero Tolerance)

The following will result in immediate removal and potential account termination:

### 2.1 Copyright Violations
- **Professional photos**: Photographer portraits, stock images, news photos you don't own
- **Music**: Songs in tribute videos without proper licensing
- **Text**: Copied obituaries, articles, or book excerpts without permission
- **Artwork**: Paintings, drawings, or designs you didn't create

**Temporary Process**: Copyright holders should email copyright@legacyvault.com with proof of ownership. We remove infringing content within 48 hours of verification. Repeat infringers will be banned.

### 2.2 Graphic and Disturbing Content
- Death imagery (bodies, autopsy photos, crime scenes)
- Graphic violence or medical trauma images
- Content intended to shock or traumatize

**Exception**: Peaceful open-casket photos with content warnings are permitted if culturally appropriate.

### 2.3 Sexual Content
- Nudity, pornography, or sexual acts
- Revenge porn (intimate images shared without consent)
- Sexualization of the deceased

### 2.4 Defamation and False Claims
- Knowingly false statements harming the deceased's reputation
- False accusations (crimes, abuse) without legal documentation
- Fabricated quotes or achievements

### 2.5 Privacy Violations
- Medical records or private diagnoses without authorization
- Financial information, addresses, phone numbers of living family
- Private messages or hacked/leaked communications
- Photos of minors without parental consent

### 2.6 Hate and Harassment
- Hate speech targeting race, religion, gender, orientation, or disability
- Content created to mock or ridicule the deceased
- Harassment of surviving family members through the memorial

### 2.7 Illegal Content
- CSAM (Child Sexual Abuse Material) — reported to law enforcement immediately
- Evidence of crimes or instructions for illegal acts
- Fraud, scams, or phishing attempts

## 3. Family Dispute Resolution (Critical)

### 3.1 The "Family War Zone" Protocol
Families often disagree about memorial content. We do not take sides. Our only goal is legal compliance and platform safety.

**Examples of disputes we see:**
- Second wife vs. adult children from first marriage
- Siblings arguing over "unflattering" photos
- Estate executor claiming memorial creator had no authority
- Ex-spouses disputing representation of marriage

### 3.2 Our Neutral Stance
**Legacy Vault does not:**
- Judge family conflicts or determine "truth"
- Mediate estate disputes
- Determine who "deserves" to control the narrative
- Provide legal advice

**Legacy Vault does:**
- Follow legal documentation (court orders, estate papers)
- Provide a 30-day dispute resolution process
- Remove content that violates this policy or law
- Protect ourselves from liability through your Memorial Authorization Form

### 3.3 Dispute Resolution Process

**Step 1: Informal Resolution (Days 1-7)**
We encourage families to resolve issues privately. Contact the memorial creator directly if possible.

**Step 2: Formal Complaint (Days 8-14)**
Email disputes@legacyvault.com with:
- Your relationship to deceased
- Specific content in dispute (URLs, screenshots)
- Legal basis (privacy rights, defamation, copyright)
- Evidence supporting your claim
- What resolution you seek (removal, editing, transfer of control)

**Step 3: Temporary Action (Days 15-21)**
- We may temporarily hide disputed content pending resolution
- Memorial creator is notified and given 7 days to respond
- If memorial creator provides legal authority (probate documents, authorization), content may be restored
- If no legal documentation provided, content remains hidden

**Step 4: Final Resolution (Days 22-30)**
- **Content stays**: If memorial creator demonstrates legal authority and content doesn't violate policy
- **Content removed**: If violation confirmed, no legal authority shown, or court order received
- **Court order required**: For complex disputes we cannot adjudicate, we require court documentation

### 3.4 Authority Hierarchy
When disputes arise, we give weight to (in order):
1. **Court orders or legal injunctions**
2. **Estate executor with probate documentation**
3. **Spouse (if legally married at death)**
4. **Adult children (majority consensus)**
5. **Parents of deceased**
6. **Memorial Authorization Form submitted at creation**

*Note: Without legal documentation, we default to the memorial creator who submitted the Authorization Form.*

### 3.5 No Liability for Family Conflicts
**You explicitly agree**: Legacy Vault is not liable for:
- Emotional distress from family disputes
- Removal or retention of disputed content
- Legal costs incurred by any party
- Damages arising from family disagreements

**Protection**: Your Memorial Authorization Form requires you to indemnify us against claims from other family members.

## 4. Post-Mortem Privacy and Dignity

### 4.1 Respect for the Deceased
- No mockery, satire, or ridicule
- No exploitation contrary to the person's known wishes
- No disclosure of private information the deceased kept confidential in life

### 4.2 Public Figures vs. Private Individuals
**Private individuals** receive higher privacy protections. **Public figures** allow broader historical discussion, but still subject to prohibitions on defamation and graphic content.

### 4.3 Cultural Sensitivity
We respect diverse mourning traditions. Content should not gratuitously violate cultural or religious norms regarding the dead.

## 5. Temporary DMCA Copyright Procedure

⚠️ **TEMPORARY PROCEDURE**: Legacy Vault has not yet registered a designated DMCA agent with the U.S. Copyright Office. The following good-faith procedure provides copyright protection until formal registration is completed.

### 5.1 Reporting Copyright Infringement
If you believe your copyrighted work is on our platform without authorization:

**Email**: copyright@legacyvault.com

**Include**:
1. Your name and contact information
2. Description of the copyrighted work (photo, music, text, etc.)
3. Exact URL where the infringing material appears
4. Statement that you have a good faith belief the use is not authorized
5. Statement that the information is accurate, under penalty of perjury
6. Your electronic or physical signature

### 5.2 Our Response
- **Review**: We assess claim within 48 hours
- **Removal**: If claim appears valid, we remove or disable access to the material
- **Notification**: We notify the user who posted the content
- **Record**: We keep records of takedowns for repeat infringer policy

### 5.3 Counter-Notification (If Your Content Was Removed)
If you believe your content was removed by mistake:

**Email**: copyright@legacyvault.com with subject "Counter-Notification"

**Include**:
1. Your name and contact information
2. Description of removed content and where it appeared
3. Statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake
4. Statement that you consent to jurisdiction of federal court in Delaware
5. Your electronic or physical signature

**Restoration**: If we receive a valid counter-notification, we may restore the content after 10 business days unless we receive notice that a lawsuit has been filed.

### 5.4 Limitations
**This temporary procedure**:
- Does not provide full DMCA safe harbor protection
- Is a good-faith effort to respect copyright rights
- Will be replaced with formal DMCA procedures upon registration of a designated agent
- Does not constitute legal advice

**For copyright holders**: If you require formal DMCA compliance immediately, please contact us to arrange expedited processing or legal coordination.

### 5.5 Repeat Infringers
Users with multiple valid copyright complaints will have their accounts terminated and memorials removed.

## 6. Reporting Violations

### 6.1 How to Report
**Email**: reports@legacyvault.com

**Include**:
- URL of memorial
- Description of violation (which policy section)
- Your relationship to content (family, rights holder, visitor)
- Evidence or explanation
- Your contact information

### 6.2 Review Process
- **Initial review**: 48 hours
- **Investigation**: We examine content against policy and context
- **Action**: Removal, warning, suspension, or no action
- **Notification**: Reporter and content owner notified (unless safety concern)

### 6.3 Appeals
**Email**: appeals@legacyvault.com within 14 days
- Explain why the decision was incorrect
- Provide additional context
- Senior team reviews within 7 days (decision is final)
