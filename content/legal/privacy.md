## 1. Introduction: A Sacred Trust

At ULUMAE, we understand that we are not merely storing data—we are safeguarding the most precious memories of your life and the lives of those you love. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information in accordance with the highest standards of digital stewardship.

We treat your memorial data with the same care and reverence that you would give to a family archive. Our commitment to privacy is inseparable from our commitment to durability: just as we promise to preserve your memories for generations, we promise to protect them with equal dedication.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create a ULUMAE account, we collect:
- **Identity**: Your name and email address
- **Authentication**: Encrypted password (hashed using bcrypt)
- **Payment Data**: Billing information processed securely through Stripe (we never store full credit card numbers on our servers)
- **Preferences**: Your communication preferences and account settings

### 2.2 Memorial Content
The heart of our service involves preserving rich biographical content:
- **Biographical Data**: Names, dates, places, life stories, career histories, family relationships
- **Media**: Photographs, videos, audio recordings, documents, and scanned materials
- **User Contributions**: Stories, memories, and tributes added by you or invited family members
- **Metadata**: Upload dates, file sizes, geolocation data (if included in uploaded files), and organizational tags

### 2.3 Usage Information
To improve our service and ensure security, we collect:
- **Technical Data**: IP addresses, browser type, device information, operating system
- **Activity Logs**: Access times, pages viewed, features used, and memorial viewing statistics
- **Cookies**: Essential session cookies and optional analytics cookies (see Section 9)

### 2.4 Concierge Service Data
For our Concierge tier ($6,500 white-glove service), we additionally collect:
- **Consultation Notes**: Information shared during Zoom calls or email correspondence
- **Processing Metadata**: Notes on how materials are organized and presented
- **Temporary Working Files**: High-resolution copies during the digitization and editing process

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Your Control Over Visibility
- **Private Memorials**: Only you and invited collaborators can view content
- **Public Memorials**: Visible to anyone with the link or found through search (you control this setting)
- **Collaborative Access**: When you invite family members via email, they gain access to contribute content to specific memorials

### 4.2 Service Providers
We share data only with trusted third parties necessary to operate our service:

| Provider | Purpose | Data Shared |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Supabase** | Database hosting and storage | All memorial content, account data |
| **Stripe** | Payment processing | Billing information, transaction history |
| **Zoom** | Concierge consultations (if chosen) | Video call metadata, recordings (if consented) |
| **Vercel** | Website hosting | Technical logs, IP addresses |

All providers are contractually obligated to use your data solely for providing their services to us and are GDPR-compliant where applicable.

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information:
- **Court Orders**: In response to valid legal process, subpoenas, or court orders
- **Protection**: When necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users
- **Fraud Prevention**: To investigate violations of our Terms or fraudulent payment activity

### 4.4 Business Transfers
If ULUMAE is acquired, merges with another entity, or sells assets, your data will transfer to the new owner subject to the same privacy commitments. We will notify you via email 30 days before any such transfer.

### 4.5 What We Never Do
**We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or memorial content to third parties for marketing purposes.** We do not use your memorial photos in advertising. We do not train AI models on your private content.

## 5. Data Retention and Deletion

### 5.1 Active Accounts
Your data remains stored as long as your account is active. We maintain:
- **Primary Storage**: Live databases with real-time access
- **Backups**: Encrypted backups retained for 90 days to enable recovery from technical failures
- **Archives**: Long-term archival storage for durability promises

### 5.2 Account Deletion
When you delete your account:
- **Grace Period**: 30 days to reverse deletion if changed your mind
- **Permanent Deletion**: After 30 days, all personal identifiers and memorial content are permanently erased from active databases
- **Backup Expiration**: Encrypted backups containing your data expire naturally within 90 days
- **Legal Retention**: Payment records retained for 7 years per tax regulations (stripped of memorial content)

### 5.3 Concierge Project Retention
For completed Concierge projects:
- **Working Files**: Temporary high-res copies and draft materials deleted 30 days after memorial publication
- **Final Deliverables**: Preserved indefinitely per your durability plan unless you delete your account
- **Communication Records**: Email threads and consultation notes retained for 2 years for quality assurance

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you have specific rights regarding your personal data:

### 6.1 For All Users (Global)
- **Access**: Request a complete copy of your data at any time
- **Correction**: Edit or update any information in your memorials
- **Export**: Download your entire memorial as a ZIP file containing HTML, JSON, and all media (guaranteed portability)
- **Deletion**: Close your account and permanently delete your data

### 6.2 GDPR Rights (EU/UK Users)
If you are in the European Union or United Kingdom:
- **Right to be Forgotten**: Request immediate erasure of personal data
- **Data Portability**: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Restriction**: Request we limit processing while disputes are resolved
- **Objection**: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Consent Withdrawal**: Revoke permission for specific processing activities

### 6.3 CCPA Rights (California Residents)
If you are a California resident:
- **Know**: Request disclosure of categories and specific pieces of personal information collected
- **Delete**: Request deletion of personal information (with certain exceptions)
- **Opt-Out**: Direct us not to sell your personal information (though we never sell data, we honor this request formally)
- **Non-Discrimination**: We will not discriminate against you for exercising privacy rights

### 6.4 Deceased Persons' Rights
We recognize that memorials often contain information about deceased individuals. While privacy laws vary by jurisdiction regarding post-mortem rights:
- **Estate Authority**: Estates or authorized representatives may request modification or removal of memorials
- **Family Disputes**: We maintain a neutral stance and may require legal documentation to resolve conflicts between family members
- **Dignity**: We reserve the right to remove content that defames the deceased or causes undue harm to surviving family members

### 6.5 How to Exercise Rights
Submit requests to: **privacy@ulumae.com**

Response time: 30 days (may extend to 60 days for complex requests). We will verify your identity before processing sensitive requests.

## 7. Security Measures

### 7.1 Technical Safeguards
- **Encryption**: AES-256 encryption for data at rest; TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- **Access Control**: Role-based access controls; staff access logged and audited
- **Infrastructure**: SOC 2 Type II certified hosting through Supabase
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring for unauthorized access attempts

### 7.2 Concierge Security Protocols
For our white-glove service:
- **Confidentiality Agreements**: All Concierge staff sign NDAs and privacy pledges
- **Device Security**: Staff work on encrypted, company-managed devices only
- **Access Limits**: Staff can only access projects assigned to them; no broad database access
- **Audit Trails**: Every access to your memorial data is logged with timestamp and action

### 7.3 Incident Response
In the unlikely event of a data breach:
- **Notification**: We will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovery
- **Transparency**: Clear communication about what data was involved and steps taken
- **Mitigation**: Immediate action to secure systems and prevent recurrence
- **Support**: Credit monitoring or identity protection services offered if sensitive financial data is involved
