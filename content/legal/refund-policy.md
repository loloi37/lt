# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

**Effective Date:** February 2, 2026

## 1. Overview

Legacy Vault creates permanent digital memorials that require significant preparation, storage allocation, and curation work. Due to the nature of digital memorialization services and the immediate costs we incur upon purchase (server provisioning, support team allocation, curation preparation), we maintain a structured refund policy that balances fairness to our customers with protection against abuse.

**By purchasing a Legacy Vault memorial, you acknowledge and agree to this Refund & Cancellation Policy.**

---

## 2. General Principles

### 2.1 Service Nature
Our memorials are **custom digital services** that include:
- Permanent cloud storage allocation
- Professional curation and formatting (Family Plan)
- Immediate server resource provisioning
- Support team assignment
- Domain/URL reservation

Once work begins or digital assets are delivered, the service is considered partially or fully consumed.

### 2.2 Cooling-Off Period
We offer a **30-day cooling-off period** for cancellations before memorial publication. After publication, the memorial is considered complete and delivered, making it ineligible for refund except in specific circumstances outlined below.

### 2.3 Family Dispute Exception
We recognize that memorialization can trigger family conflicts. We provide a **Family Dispute Refund** pathway that supersedes standard policy when valid legal documentation is provided.

## 3. Personal Plan Refunds ($300)

### 3.1 Before Publication (Draft Status)
- **Full refund** available within 30 days of purchase if memorial has not been published
- **Cancellation**: You may cancel anytime before publication for full refund
- **Processing time**: 5-7 business days to original payment method

### 3.2 After Publication (Live Status)
- **No refunds** once memorial is published and live
- **Reason**: Digital product delivered, storage allocated, URL active
- **Transfer option**: You may transfer ownership to another family member instead of cancellation

### 3.3 Partial Work Exception
If you request significant customization beyond standard templates before publication:
- **Over 2 hours of custom work**: 50% refund only (remaining 50% covers labor costs)
- **Under 2 hours of custom work**: Full refund under 3.1

---

## 4. Family Plan Refunds ($3,000)

### 4.1 Before Curation Begins
- **Full refund** available if curation team has not started work
- **Status check**: Email refunds@legacyvault.com to confirm work status
- **Timeline**: Typically 5 business days after purchase if no work started

### 4.2 During Curation (Work in Progress)
- **50% refund** if curation has begun but memorial not yet published
- **Calculation**: Covers labor costs already incurred by curation team
- **Cancellation**: Memorial will not be completed or published

### 4.3 After Publication
- **No refunds** once Family Plan memorial is published
- **Deliverables**: Includes professional curation, custom design, family tree setup, permanent hosting
- **Service complete**: Work is considered fully delivered upon publication

### 4.4 Multi-Payment/Installment Plans
If you selected installment payments:
- **Cancellation before completion**: Cease future payments, no refund of prior payments if work has begun
- **Cancellation before work starts**: Full refund of payments made
- **Default**: If you stop paying installments, memorial remains in draft status until paid in full or cancelled per 4.1

## 5. Family Dispute Exception (All Plans)

### 5.1 Eligibility
If a family dispute arises regarding the memorial with **valid legal documentation**:

**Qualifying documentation**:
- Court order regarding estate authority
- Cease and desist letter from licensed attorney representing estate
- Documentation proving memorial creator lacked legal authority
- Evidence of fraudulent purchase (identity theft, unauthorized card use)

### 5.2 Refund Terms
- **Full refund** issued to original purchaser
- **Memorial removal**: Complete deletion of all content
- **No penalties**: Family dispute refunds do not count against refund limits
- **Timeline**: Processed within 10 business days of documentation verification

### 5.3 Process
1. Email disputes@legacyvault.com with documentation
2. Our team verifies legal validity (may require attorney consultation)
3. If valid, refund issued and memorial removed within 72 hours
4. If insufficient documentation, standard refund policy applies

### 5.4 No Documentation Refunds
Without legal documentation, family disagreements do **not** qualify for exception refunds. You must use our Content Policy dispute resolution process instead.

## 6. Cancellation Process

### 6.1 How to Cancel
Email **refunds@legacyvault.com** with:
- Account email address
- Memorial URL or name
- Reason for cancellation
- Confirmation you understand refund terms

### 6.2 Timeline
- **Response**: Within 2 business days
- **Refund processing**: 5-10 business days after approval
- **Method**: Original payment method (credit card, bank account)

### 6.3 What Happens After Cancellation
- **Before publication**: All data deleted, URL released, no trace remains
- **After publication**: Not applicable (no cancellations accepted)
- **Family Plan mid-work**: Draft content deleted, curation stops

### 6.4 Reactivation
Cancelled memorials can be reactivated within 30 days by repurchasing at original price. After 30 days, data is permanently purged and cannot be recovered.

## 7. Non-Refundable Circumstances

### 7.1 No Refunds For:
- **Policy violations**: Accounts terminated for Content Policy violations (hate speech, copyright, illegal content)
- **Change of mind**: After publication, "I don't want it anymore" is not grounds for refund
- **Technical issues on user end**: Browser compatibility, user device problems
- **Failure to use**: Not logging in or not adding content
- **Price changes**: If prices drop after your purchase

### 7.2 Chargebacks Prohibited
**You agree not to initiate chargebacks** with your credit card company or bank without first contacting us and allowing 10 business days to resolve the issue.

**Chargeback consequences**:
- Account immediate termination
- Memorial removal
- Collection proceedings for chargeback fees ($25-100 per dispute)
- Ban from future service

**If you have a legitimate dispute**: Contact refunds@legacyvault.com first. We resolve 95% of issues without chargebacks.

## 9. Special Circumstances

### 9.1 Death of Purchaser
If the person who purchased the memorial dies before publication:
- **Estate may request**: Full refund to estate or transfer to family member
- **Documentation**: Death certificate and proof of estate authority required
- **Timeframe**: Must request within 1 year of death

### 9.2 Technical Failure on Our End
If Legacy Vault experiences catastrophic technical failure preventing memorial publication:
- **Your choice**: Full refund OR wait for resolution + service credit
- **Definition**: Server outage >30 days, data loss on our end (not user error)
- **Force Majeure**: Natural disasters, acts of war, internet infrastructure failure do not qualify

### 9.3 Fraudulent Purchases
If you did not authorize the purchase (identity theft, stolen card):
- **Immediate refund** upon verification
- **Cooperation required**: You must file police report and provide case number
- **Account security**: We will help secure the account
